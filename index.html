Tornado Cash - DeFi Protocol for Private Transactions on Ethereum
Tornado Cash - Secure Your Financial Privacy with Decentralized Anonymity.

Tornado Cash is a decentralized, non-custodial protocol that enables private transactions on the Ethereum blockchain. By using zero-knowledge proofs (zk-SNARKs), it ensures complete anonymity by breaking the on-chain link between sender and receiver addresses.

The protocol incentivizes liquidity by allowing users to deposit ETH or ERC20 tokens into pools, which are mixed with other deposits. Users receive a private note to withdraw funds to a new address, ensuring no traceable connection between deposits and withdrawals.

For enhanced privacy, Tornado Cash leverages decentralized relayers and advanced cryptographic techniques. The protocol is fully open-source and has been audited by leading security firms to ensure trust and reliability.


What is Tornado Cash?

Tornado Cash is a decentralized protocol designed for private transactions on Ethereum. It uses zk-SNARKs to provide anonymity, allowing users to deposit and withdraw funds without revealing their transaction history. Built on Ethereum, it operates as a smart contract, making it non-custodial and trustless.

The protocol rewards liquidity providers by allowing relayers to facilitate withdrawals for a small fee. This ensures the system remains decentralized and operational without relying on a central authority.

To ensure robust privacy, Tornado Cash employs advanced cryptographic tools and decentralized relayers. The system is designed to minimize traceability, providing users with a secure way to protect their financial privacy.
